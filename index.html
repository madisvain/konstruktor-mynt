Konstruktor

We create web solutions
that help your business thrive.

Konstruktor is a small team that creates smart web software solutions to help companies improve efficiency and financial performance. We are really passionate about what we do and believe that web-based technologies will change the way businesses think and operate. We work closely with our clients
to create meaningful websites and well adaptable online products. Konstruktor also develops web apps, our latest beta stage apps include simple online invoicing tool InvoiceClerk for freelancers and small businesses and a budget management tool Budgetto that helps companies keep costs under control.
